Project - The Original Campus Tracking Services System

aws.iotree.com/campus2 TryMe
github.com/c-tham/django_CampusService2
1518 lines of code (HTML: 785 lines, Python: 733 lines)
Bootstrap 3
Development: May 26 to 28, 2018

The Original Campus (aka Campus 2) tracks a persons use of services like building and office acccess, meal cards, and parking permits. It also keeps track of addresses, phone numbers, emails, and office extension numbers.

When a person registers to the system, it will generate a college email address. The college email address will be the main login information. The password is stored using bcrypt which it's generating a salt to protect against rainbow table attacks.

The system is able to generate unique college ID, unique college email address and unique parking permit ID. I have listed a college ID (under CheatSheet) in the main page for us to login. You can copy and paste it on the login field.

Please feel free to try out my applications. Please share your thoughts by giving feedback on the form at the top right of menu bar. If you found a bug, please do not hesitate to let me know.

Please use fake information when you registrate to the system. Thanks!
